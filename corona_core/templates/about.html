This webapp was created with the goal of curbing the spread of misinformation regarding coronavirus.

It is primarily targeted towards regional language users in tier 3 cities and beyond. They need access to facts, but don't have a reliable source.

News reports, healthcare guidelines and research papers have a tendency to use language that is hard to interpret by inexperienced readers.

Few points:
  • We share few facts, but share correct/verified facts. Currently, our only source of information is a translation of: the WHO Q&A on covid-19
  • We try our best to explain using simple terms, in a language that is easy to understand
  • Currently we are bootstrapping off google translate, but we need translators to work actively with us for a short duration. If you are interested in volunteering, please email us at admin@tildehat.com
  • We try to minimize the footprint of the website, so it loads with a slow internet connection
