The goal is to convey the basics of coronavirus to most regional language users in small cities/villages of India. Literacy is not a barrier, as the app uses a text to speech interface to read the content aloud. News reports, healthcare guidelines and research papers have a tendency to use language that is hard to interpret by inexperienced readers. A secondary goal is to curb the spread of misinformation and superstition regarding the virus.

The philosophy:
  • We share few facts, but they are correct/verified facts. Currently, our only source of information is a translation of: the WHO Q&A on covid-19
  • We try our best to explain using simple terms, in a language that is easy to understand
  • We try to minimize the footprint of the website, so that it loads with a slow internet connection
